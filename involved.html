Get Involved

The lack of women in tech begins at an educational level. “From an early age, girls have been steered away from careers and activities having to do with STEM", “And even the ones that have shown interest have not been encouraged to pursue it”(Forbes).

Even if you are not working in tech, you can educate yourself with the following links in order to be more aware about this issue and its implications. If we can raise a generation of young people who know the societal risks of our technology, we may be able to make way for a more inclusive future in tech.

Technological Development

Why should you care that women make up less than 20% of ICT (Information and Communications Technology) students ? Why should society be bothered that women make up only 22% of software developers ?
The answer is simple, 90% of our lives, our education, our careers, and our healthcare are developed using software. On average, we spend 415.5 minutes every day online (as of 2022) (Kemp). That means 415.5 minutes a day using software that is developed by men 80% of the time. When software is developed by men, it is not only less useful for women, less visually appealing for women, but it can be extremely damaging for women's well-being as well.
In 2014 Apple, the world's biggest Tech company, released the Apple Watch, along with the Apple Watch health app and many others. The health app “is a powerful app. It allows users to track everything from calories to electrodermal activity to heart rate to blood alcohol content to respiratory rate to daily intake of chromium. But there’s a notable exception. Apple Health doesn’t track menstruation” (Eveleth).
Considering about half of the world population is menstruating for a compiled ten years of their life, this seems like a huge piece of women's health that is being ignored.

“Advances in technology will surely not consider women’s issues if there are no women to point them in that direction” - Somi Arian
